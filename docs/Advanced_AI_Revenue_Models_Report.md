# MODEL 1: Rank & Rent SEO (AI-Powered Lead Generation Sites)

## Overview
Build and rank local business websites using AI SEO automation, then rent them to businesses for monthly recurring revenue. This is essentially "digital real estate"—create valuable online properties and collect rent.

**Revenue Potential:** $500-$3,000+ per site per month
**Automation Level:** 80% after initial ranking
**Starting Cost:** $100-500/site
**Time to Revenue:** 3-6 months per site

---

## How It Works

1. Create a local service website (e.g., "Emergency Plumbing Omaha")
2. Use AI to generate SEO-optimized content
3. Rank on Google for local keywords
4. Collect leads (calls, form submissions)
5. Rent the site to a local business for $500-3,000/month
6. Or sell leads individually for $20-100 each

---

## Step-by-Step Implementation

### Phase 1: Niche Research (Days 1-7)

**Best Niches for Rank & Rent:**

| Category | Example Keywords | Typical Rent |
|----------|-----------------|--------------|
| Home Services | "tree removal [city]", "roofing [city]" | $500-1,500/mo |
| Professional | "DUI lawyer [city]", "dentist [city]" | $1,000-3,000/mo |
| Emergency | "24/7 locksmith [city]", "emergency plumber [city]" | $750-2,000/mo |
| Specialty | "concrete contractor [city]", "bathroom remodeling [city]" | $500-1,000/mo |

**Niche Selection Criteria:**
- Service value $500+ (businesses can afford rent)
- Local competition not dominated by big brands
- Clear intent (people searching need service now)
- No major YMYL (Your Money Your Life) issues

### Phase 2: Site Setup (Days 8-14)

**Essential Components:**

1. **Domain**
   - Exact match or partial match keyword
   - Example: "OmahaPlumbingPros.com"
   - Cost: $10-15/year

2. **Hosting**
   - Basic shared hosting works
   - Cost: $5-10/month

3. **Website Platform**
   - WordPress (free) with lightweight theme
   - Or use AI site builders

4. **Google Business Profile**
   - Create legitimate local listing
   - Get virtual office or use your address
   - Verify via postcard

### Phase 3: AI Content Generation (Weeks 3-4)

**Content Strategy:**

Use AI tools to generate:
- 10-20 service pages targeting different keywords
- 20-50 blog posts for long-tail traffic
- FAQ pages
- Location-specific landing pages

**AI Tools:**
- **RankAI.ai**: AI SEO agency that creates 20+ pages/month
- **SEOWriting.ai**: Generate SEO articles
- **ChatGPT/Claude**: Custom prompts for content

**Content Structure:**
```
Homepage: Main service + city
├── Service 1: "Plumbing Repair [City]"
├── Service 2: "Drain Cleaning [City]"
├── Service 3: "Water Heater [City]"
├── Location Pages: Each suburb/neighborhood
└── Blog: Tips, guides, local news
```

### Phase 4: Ranking & Lead Generation (Months 2-6)

**SEO Strategy:**

1. **On-Page Optimization**
   - Target keywords in titles, headers, content
   - Optimize meta descriptions
   - Internal linking structure

2. **Off-Page SEO**
   - Build local citations (Yelp, Yellow Pages, etc.)
   - Get local backlinks
   - Create Google Business Profile posts

3. **Technical SEO**
   - Fast loading speed
   - Mobile-friendly
   - Schema markup

**Timeline:**
- Months 1-2: Build content, get indexed
- Months 2-4: Start ranking for long-tail keywords
- Months 4-6: Rank for main keywords, generate leads

### Phase 5: Monetization

**Option A: Rent the Entire Site**
- Fixed monthly fee ($500-3,000)
- Transfer all calls/leads to one business
- Hands-off after setup

**Option B: Pay-Per-Lead**
- Charge $20-100 per qualified lead
- Use call tracking software
- Higher upside but more management

**Option C: Sell the Site**
- Once established (10+ leads/month)
- Sell for 20-40x monthly revenue
- $10,000-50,000+ exit
